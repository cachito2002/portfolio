FOR YOU

OK, so this page will be a little bit more detailed than the other ones. This is going to be a customized page; what this means is that there is going to be some pictures (they will be clickable in the future), there will be a picture for a link for each section. The sections will be: Logos, Customs designs (painting), Web Design or Choose for yourself. Each section will lead to another page. Imagine the wireframe cc but more complicated (hints why the following additions will be for the future). If you were to click on the logo one it would pop up a square link frame with two options, to either contact me or ask feedback for a design you have and have a video call to help you improve it. The custom design one will pop you into another page where there are 4 pictures that are also clickable. One would be a shirt( or hoodie), shoes, hydroflask, denim jacket. When one is chosen a tool bar would be chosen, as you put start to put things on the shirt or shoe you would be able to see what it would look like. Since this is just a rough draft of the persons design the next step would be to confirm their design. I would from my end then proceed to design the shirt or shoe. If they picked a shoe I would contact them and ask for what they would like. I tell them about how long it would take. I would sketch out some colored drawings so the person can see what their design would look like. (Send them through email and talk with them about the proceedure). If they confirm the design then I would proceed to design it. This all seems complicated in words but when I do my wireframe it will all make sense.
